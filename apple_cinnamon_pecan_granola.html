Apple Cinnamon Pecan Raisin Granola

Ingredients:

  • Rolled oats – 18 oz
  • Walnuts – 1/2 cup
  • Pecans – 1/2 cup
  • Dried apples (chopped) – 2 oz
  • Raisins – 1/2 cup
  • Maple syrup – 1/3 cup
  • Avocado oil – 1/4 cup
  • Ground cinnamon – 2 tsp
  • Vanilla extract – 1 tsp
  • Sea salt – 1/4 tsp

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix oats, walnuts, pecans, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Combine maple syrup, oil, and vanilla; warm slightly and stir into dry ingredients.
  4. Spread mixture on pan, press down, and bake 40–45 minutes.
  5. Cool completely, break into clusters, and mix in dried apples and raisins.
