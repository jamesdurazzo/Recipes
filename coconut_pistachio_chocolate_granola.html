Coconut Pistachio Dark Chocolate Granola

Ingredients:

  • Rolled oats – 18 oz
  • Sliced almonds – 1/2 cup
  • Pistachios (chopped) – 1/2 cup
  • Unsweetened coconut flakes – 3/4 cup
  • Dried berries – 1.2 oz
  • Maple syrup – 1/3 cup
  • Avocado oil – 1/4 cup
  • Vanilla extract – 1 tsp
  • Sea salt – 1/4 tsp
  • Dark chocolate (chopped) – 3.5 oz

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix oats, almonds, pistachios, coconut, and salt.
  3. Combine maple syrup, oil, and vanilla; warm and mix with dry ingredients.
  4. Spread on pan, press down, and bake 40–45 minutes.
  5. Cool, break into clusters, and stir in dried berries and chocolate.
